- Level 2: Agentblazer Innovator ✅ (User has this)

- User has 110 badges ✅

**Corrections Made:**
- Removed "top 5% globally" from all instances
- Removed "top 0.1% globally" from all instances
- Kept factual achievements: "110 Trailhead Badges (Ranger Rank)"

**User Response:** "It's ok, that is why we work as a team."

**Final Accurate Language:**
- "110 Trailhead Badges (Ranger Rank)"
- "Completed 110 Salesforce Trailhead badges in 9 months achieving Ranger rank"
- "Agentblazer Champion & Innovator - Early adopter of Salesforce Agentforce AI technology"

**Lesson Learned:** Always verify claims with official sources. Team collaboration (user verification + AI correction) ensures 100% accuracy.
